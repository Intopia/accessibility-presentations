Developing for web accessibility

Back to Education and learning

Ready to become an accessibility coding boss? Expand your knowledge of ARIA, the accessibility tree, building accessible forms and web components, and heaps more. This course is great for front-end or full-stack developers.

What you’ll learn

  • The importance of the accessibility tree, accessible names and descriptions.
  • The correct use of semantic markup for headings, landmarks, and images.
  • How to use ARIA â€“ the right way.
  • How to create accessible form markup, including required fields and errors.
  • How to provide dynamic content to assistive technologies.
  • How to build accessible notifications and custom components.

Knowledge level

  • Basic knowledge of HTML is required.
  • No prior accessibility knowledge is needed.

Technology requirements

We use CodePen for some activities. You don't need an account, but it will allow you to fork and save examples.

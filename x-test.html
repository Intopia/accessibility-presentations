Free “Introduction to digital accessibility” session

Session duration: 1 x 60-minute sessions

Take a crash course in digital accessibility – what it’s about, and why it matters. We’ll look at assistive technology in use; developing user profiles for accessibility; and roles and responsibilities for team members when developing accessible products. Let’s get started building an accessible digital world.

What you’ll learn

  • Why should you care about accessibility?
  • Videos of different types of assistive technologies
  • A discussion on the value of accessibility user profiles
  • An overview of accessibility roles and responsibilities within teams
  • Questions and discussion

Who should attend

This course is great for anyone in your team!

Knowledge level

No prior accessibility knowledge needed.

Technology requirements

We use online tools such as FigJam to work together during this course. You won’t need to install or create an account to use these tools.
